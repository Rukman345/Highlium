ABOUT

Our Origin

Highlium was created from the consulting and market intelligence roots of Greenox Gases, a company actively involved in industrial gases, clean energy, and sustainability solutions. As the gas industry evolved and becoming more global, capital-intensive, and technology-driven; the need for independent strategy and intelligence became critical. Highlium was established as a focused, standalone platform dedicated to advisory, intelligence, and investment support for the industrial gas ecosystem.

Highlium carries deep operational understanding from real industry experience, combined with structured consulting and research capability.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted strategy and intelligence partner for the global industrial gas and energy transition ecosystem.

Our Mission

  • • Deliver accurate, structured, and decision-grade market intelligence
  • • Enable profitable and sustainable gas investments
  • • Support companies in strategy, pricing, and market entry
  • • Build platforms that connect industry, investors, and technology
  • • Accelerate the growth of Helium and Hydrogen markets worldwide

Why Highlium Exists

The industrial gas industry is entering a new phase driven by energy transition, capital expansion, technology change, and stricter regulation. Decisions today involve large investments, long payback periods, and high strategic risk.

Companies and investors no longer need just data, but they need insight, interpretation, and clear strategic direction. Highlium exists to bridge the gap between information and action, turning market signals into business decisions and investment confidence.

What Makes Highlium Different

  • • Deep specialization in industrial gases rather than general consulting
  • • Clear leadership in Helium and Hydrogen advisory
  • • Combination of consulting, market intelligence, and industry platforms
  • • Strong China and Asia intelligence through Gas Ecosphere partnership
  • • Business-first, practical advisory grounded in industry realities
