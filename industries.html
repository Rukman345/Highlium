INDUSTRIES SERVED

Highlium works across the full industrial gas and energy transition ecosystem, supporting both supply-side players and end-user industries.

Industrial Gas Producers and Distributors

We support gas producers and distributors with strategy, market intelligence, pricing, expansion, and investment planning across bulk, packaged, and onsite models.

EPCs and Plant Builders

We work with EPCs and technology providers to support project development, feasibility, market positioning, and client acquisition for gas plants and energy projects.

Energy Companies

We advise energy companies on diversification into industrial gases, hydrogen, LNG, and energy transition projects through market analysis, investment strategy, and commercial structuring.

Investors, Funds, and Developers

We support private equity, infrastructure funds, family offices, and project developers with due diligence, market intelligence, feasibility studies, and investment risk analysis.

Governments and Regulators

We work with ministries, regulators, and state entities to support gas policy, market development, investment attraction, and infrastructure planning.

Manufacturing and Heavy Industry

We support large industrial users that depend on oxygen, nitrogen, hydrogen, argon, and specialty gases for operations, cost control, and sustainability goals.

Healthcare and Medical Gases

We advise hospitals, healthcare groups, and gas suppliers on medical oxygen, specialty medical gases, supply security, and regulatory compliance.

Technology and Equipment Suppliers

We support manufacturers of ASUs, electrolyzers, compressors, storage systems, cylinders, valves, and cryogenic equipment with market entry and growth strategy.

End-User Industries

Highlium connects industrial gas intelligence with the industries that depend on it—supporting growth, efficiency, and energy transition across the global economy.

Electronics and Semiconductors

We work with semiconductor fabs and electronics manufacturers that rely on ultra-high-purity gases such as nitrogen, hydrogen, helium, and specialty gases for chip fabrication, display manufacturing, and advanced electronics.

Oil and Gas

We support upstream, midstream, and downstream oil and gas companies using industrial gases for refining, processing, enhanced recovery, and hydrogen-based upgrading.

Energy and Power

We advise power producers and utilities using hydrogen, oxygen, nitrogen, and LNG for generation, storage, grid balancing, and transition planning.

Aviation and Aerospace

We work with aerospace manufacturers, airports, and space programs using oxygen, nitrogen, and helium for aircraft systems, space launch, testing, and cryogenic applications.

Chemicals and Petrochemicals

We support chemical and petrochemical producers using hydrogen, nitrogen, oxygen, and specialty gases for synthesis, processing, and emissions reduction.

Metals, Steel, and Mining

We advise steel plants, aluminum smelters, and mining companies using oxygen, nitrogen, and argon for smelting, refining, cutting, and safety systems.

Cement and Construction Materials

We support cement and materials producers using oxygen enrichment, CO₂ capture, and industrial gases for efficiency, emissions reduction, and alternative fuels.

Food and Beverage

We advise food processors and beverage companies using CO₂, nitrogen, and oxygen for carbonation, packaging, preservation, and freezing.

Pharmaceuticals and Biotechnology

We work with pharma and biotech companies using high-purity gases for synthesis, fermentation, storage, and laboratory applications.

Data Centers and Digital Infrastructure

We support data centers using nitrogen, helium, and cooling gases for fire suppression, cooling, and reliability systems.

Automotive and Mobility

We advise automotive manufacturers and mobility companies using industrial gases for welding, coating, testing, and hydrogen mobility.

Research, Space, and Advanced Science

We work with research institutions, space agencies, and laboratories using helium, hydrogen, and specialty gases for cryogenics, testing, and innovation.
